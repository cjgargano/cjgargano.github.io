Hi, I'm Chris Gargano

I am an aspiring Data Scientist with an educational background in Applied Statistics and career experiences in Commercial Real Estate and Marketing Analytics.


I graduated from a 5-year M.S. program in Applied Statistics from Penn State in 2012. Upon graduation, I worked in New York for one year as an Analyst for Marketing Management Analytics, which specializes in Marketing Mix Modeling.


In the summer of 2013, I moved to Orange County, CA to work at Altus Group, a management consulting firm in the Commercial Real Estate industry. My role at Altus has evolved to the point where I am now part of a 3-person team specializing in Performance Analytics and Valuation Analysis.


This page is still a work-in-progress, but it will soon be updated with additional links to my Github profile, Kaggle competitions, and other Data Science projects.
